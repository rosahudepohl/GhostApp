### Ghost ###

**Ghost Words** is an addictive game of word strategy and tactics where you can use the power of vocabulary to outsmart & outplay friends & foes alike. 

### Ghost Controllers ###

##### MainActivity.java #####

**Bestaat uit**:

- Play Game --> NewGame.java
- Highscores --> Highscores.java
